中央大學學生會是什麼?

What is the NCU Student Association

學生會是中央大學的一個自治組織，我們存在的目的是為了維護學生權益、並且促進學校進步、培養民主素養以及擴大對於社會關懷。學生會致力於為學生提供一個平台，讓大家都能夠參與學校事務、發表意見、並參與決策過程。透過各項活動與計劃，我們努力推動學生參與校園生活的積極性，同時也與校方密切合作，共同打造更加開放、民主與透明的校園文化。

The NCUSA (National Central University Student Association) is an autonomous organization at NCU (National Central University). Our purpose is to safeguard student rights, promote school advancement, cultivate democratic literacy, and expand social concern. The NCUSA is committed to providing a platform for students to participate in school affairs, express opinions, and engage in decision-making processes. Through various activities and programs, we strive to promote students' active involvement in campus life. Additionally, we collaborate closely with the university administration to create a more open, democratic, and transparent campus culture.
